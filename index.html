My Projects

MithrilBot [WIP]

Does one simple thing- returns the mithril collection of any one player.

This simple bot is great for discords of mining guilds. Our team is working on a "Mithril weight" feature, which weighs a player's mining collections, HOTM experience, Mining Skill experience and other things to determine their Weight, which you can utilize to set guild requirements.



Quick Paper Minecraft Server

Setting up a server that runs 24/7 on a VPS is always complicated, especially with mods in the mix. With QPMS, the whole ordeal will be over in 30 minutes.

The function of QPMS is very simple- to ease your minecraft-server-setting-up experience to the point where it's no hassle to set it up for friends and family. the package is for Debian/Ubuntu systems, and while I'm still working on tutorials for setting it up with Oracle Cloud and Azure, and also support for Windows, but the pain of messing around with file trees and fidgeting with the settings dissapears- you can do it on your own computer, in your own code editor, in your own time and once you're on your VPS server, cloning your customized repo is a breeze.



Gogetter CLI

A small beginner project in Golang. It's not finished and probably never will be but it's there anyways.

This project was supposed to be a simple package manager that utilized a simple form of wget, but it devolved into an industrial-level solution for helping users download your package. for that reason it won't ever be fully finished but once I finish learning go and maybe can call myself an expert, I'll maybe come back to it and finish the original, smaller project.
